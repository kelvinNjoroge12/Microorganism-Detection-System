About Our System

Our Technology

The Pathogen Detection System utilizes cutting-edge deep learning algorithms to identify potentially harmful microorganisms in food samples. The AI model was trained on 14,400 microscopic images to achieve its current accuracy.

Food Safety

Early detection of harmful microorganisms can prevent foodborne illnesses and improve overall food safety standards. The system can help food producers, restaurants, and regulatory agencies maintain the highest safety standards.

Continuous Improvement

The AI model can continuously be updated with new data to improve detection capabilities. The system learns from each analysis, becoming more accurate over time.
